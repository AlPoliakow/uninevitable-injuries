Inevitable injuries

Shoulder injury

Mechanism of injury

Rapid fall whilst cycling onto anterior aspect of shoulder with forceful interior rotation and depression.

Symptoms:

  • Pain!!!
  • Inability to flex past 45 degrees
  • Inability to externally rotate past neutral

Tests:

  • Range of movement:~40 degrees flexion, external roation to neutral, internal rotation to inline with body
  • Infraspinatus:positive
  • Hand behind back:decreased!!!
  • Full can:decreased!!!
  • Empty can:decreased!!!
  • Resisted external rotation:decreased!!!
  • Neer's test:decreased!!!
  • Hornblower:positive
  • Drop-arm test:positive
  • O'Brians:negative
  • Crank's test:negative
  • Lift off test:big difference
  • Resisted external rotation:decreased!!!
  • Belly press test:
  • Ultrasound:all rotator cuff tendons intact, subacromial bursa swelling, posterior labrum swelling

Diagnosis:

Shoulder bursitis

Posterior labrum inflammation

Grade 1-2 Teres Minor tear

Treatment plan

  • Rest
  • Return to pain free ROM

Return to sport

  • Normal clinical examination
  • Normal shoulder arthokinetics
  • Normal kinetic chain integration
  • Completed exercise progressions
